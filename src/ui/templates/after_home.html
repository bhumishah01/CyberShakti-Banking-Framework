Why This Solves the Rural Problem

  • Offline-first: data stored locally in SQLite, not over the internet.
  • Fraud controls: risk scoring + hold/block + trusted contact patterns.
  • Integrity: encrypted payloads + audit chain checks.
  • Low bandwidth: sync queue with retries; server supports compressed sync v2.
